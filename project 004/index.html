DOXER - A cautionary web art project that probes the vulnerabilities of personal data exposure in this AI-driven era. Leveraging face-api.js and images from Pexels, DOXER crafts an interactive scenario where AI algorithms simulate the doxxing process, blending real-time facial detection with artificially generated personal data. This exhibition starkly illustrates how effortlessly digital identities can be created and manipulated, urging viewers to confront the realities of data safety in a world dominated by artificial intelligence. Through visual narratives, DOXER invites a critical examination of the thin line between authenticity and fabrication, encouraging a broader dialogue on privacy and the ethical challenges of AI technologies in shaping our digital lives.
